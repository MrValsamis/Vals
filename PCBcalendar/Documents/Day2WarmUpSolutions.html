Momentum and Circular Motion Day 2 Warm-Up PCB Solutions
  1. An enterprising student builds two potato launchers to further study projectile motion. The student builds one launcher with a long barrel and one with a short barrel. If the launchers both exert the same force on a potato, which launcher will send the potato farther after launching?
 Solution
The longer barrel launcher. If the same force is applied for a longer amount of time, the change in momentum (and velocity) will be greater.
  1. 350 k g 350 k g 350kg350 \mathrm{~kg}350 kg Groot and 25 k g 25 k g 25kg25 \mathrm{~kg}25 kg Rocket Raccoon are standing at rest, facing each other in the center of a frozen pond. They push their hands together, causing them to move apart.
    a. After pushing apart, who has a larger magnitude of momentum?
    b. After pushing apart, who is moving faster?
 Solution
a. They apply the same magnitude of force on each other (Newton's 3rd) for the same amount of time. They will have the same magnitude of momentum after this interaction.
b. They have the same magnitude of momentum. However, since Rocket Racoon has less mass, he will have a great amount of velocity.
